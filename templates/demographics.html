Key Demographic Insights

Analyzing employee demographics is vital for making strategic decisions on workforce development, diversity, and retention efforts. Here are some insights based on the data:

  • Average employee age is 35 years, indicating a mid-career workforce.
  • Balanced gender ratio helps maintain diversity.
  • Most employees have completed higher education in fields like Engineering and Business.
  • 70% of employees report a positive work-life balance.

Understanding these factors allows management to create targeted programs for professional development and employee satisfaction.
